About My Topic

Not every artist can support themselves solely from their art. This becomes truer the more niche the art genre is. In a music industry oversaturated with mainstream festival acts, how do artists with a passion to innovate sustain themselves? We hope to be a bridge, helping our community of artists survive while giving them the tools they need to make their music stand out.

What is food insecurity?

report on hunger-in-america

While food insecurity remains a prevalent issue in may communities around the the US, a lot of marginalized communities such as people of color and LGBTQ+ communities are at higher risk of food insecurity as a result of discrimination and poverty. The elctronic music scene has been a source of expression for such communities since the late 1960s, and a lot of music we know and love today would not have existed without such a space.
